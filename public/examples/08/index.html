Example 8. Collections of Models with Child-Views.

Auto-magic lists of Child Views

Some lists are simple, static, and are well represented by an hbs #each statement.

Others lists are much more complex. Collections of models with rich-data, interactivity; lists that might change order or membership are what we're addressing in this example.

This example jumps up quite a bit in complexity as we bring things together. We have a few different views that interact in novel ways:

  • Person.View This is our 'root' view. It contains all other views.
  • Address.DetailView This view allows you to see and edit the details of an address.
  • Address.ItemView This view appears as many times as there are addresses for the person.
